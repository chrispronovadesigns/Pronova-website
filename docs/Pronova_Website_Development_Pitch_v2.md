## The WordPress question

"Why WordPress instead of Wix or Squarespace?"

Fair question. Here's the honest answer:

**WordPress powers 43% of the entire internet** — and for good reason. It gives you complete control over your site. You're not renting space on someone else's platform — you own it. Your design, your data, your hosting, your rules.

With Wix or Squarespace, you're locked into their ecosystem. You can't move your site without rebuilding it from scratch. Their "drag and drop" simplicity comes at the cost of flexibility, performance, and SEO capability. For a personal blog, they're fine. For a business that depends on being found online, they're a ceiling you'll hit fast.

Custom WordPress gives you the visual polish of a premium site with the technical backbone of a real web application. You get a CMS you can use yourself — adding pages, updating content, swapping images — without calling a developer every time.

And when you're ready to add marketing, lead capture, e-commerce, or integrations — WordPress scales with you. That's not true of the template-based platforms.
